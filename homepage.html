ABOUT US

In the presence of eminent nature and the natural surrounding,
we present a website to help those who are daring and adventurous enough to
explore sheltered willows, growing cattails and breath infinite unpolluted fresh air.
In a new digital era, we are all too focused to be up to date that we forget to look up
from our screens to realise the inheritance and essence of what mother nature has to offer.
Besides, exploring and indulging in flora and fauna can soothe the mind and tense body due to
accumulative stress that we build up in our lives. We offer solutions and encourage people to not
only wallow in therapeutic escapes, but involve oneself in outdoor activities all over Malaysia.
